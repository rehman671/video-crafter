Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating video ads with VideoCrafter

How is this different from Canva or other video editors?

VideoCrafter automates the tedious parts of video editing (cropping, timing clips to voiceovers, syncing subtitles) that you'd have to do manually in other tools. You just highlight words and assign clips - we handle the rest. Plus, our auto-cropping to different dimensions is unmatched.

Can I add images to my videos?

Absolutely! Our platform makes it incredibly easy to incorporate images into your video ads. Simply upload your images, and VideoCrafter will automatically animate them and integrate them seamlessly into your video timeline.

Can I use my own voiceovers?

Yes! You can integrate with ElevenLabs for AI voiceovers. Our system will automatically sync your clips to the audio.

What if I don't have video clips to use?

We provide AI-powered clip suggestions from Storyblocks and Pexels. Just click to download and add them to your project. You can also create videos entirely from your image library.

Is there a limit to how many videos I can create?

The free plan includes 1 export per month. Growth plans offer 25 exports per month. While the Pro plan offers 60 exports per month.

How easy is it for virtual assistants to use?

VideoCrafter was designed specifically for non-technical users. The highlight-and-assign workflow is incredibly intuitive. Teams have their trained VAs with no prior experience to create professional videos in under 60 minutes.
